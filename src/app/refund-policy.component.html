Refund Policy

Thank you for using MentorStreak. Please read our refund policy carefully before making any course purchase. This policy applies to all students purchasing courses on the MentorStreak platform.

Overview

MentorStreak operates a course marketplace where students purchase access to courses created by course creators. Due to the digital nature of course content and the immediate access provided upon purchase, we have implemented a strict refund policy with limited exceptions.

1. Course Purchases - No Refund Policy

All course sales are final. Once you have purchased a course and gained access to the course content, we do not offer refunds, returns, or exchanges.

  • Courses are digital products delivered immediately upon purchase.
  • By purchasing a course, you acknowledge that you have had the opportunity to review the course preview, description, creator profile, and student reviews before making your purchase decision.
  • Your decision to purchase cannot be reversed.
  • No refunds will be issued for change of mind, change in personal circumstances, or dissatisfaction with course content.

2. Limited Exceptions to No Refund Policy

Refunds may be considered only in the following exceptional circumstances, at MentorStreak's sole discretion:

  • Duplicate Charges: If you are charged twice for the same course due to a technical error.
  • Unauthorized Purchase: If your account was compromised and a course was purchased without your authorization. You must report this within 7 days of the unauthorized charge.
  • Course Removed: If the creator removes or deletes the entire course shortly after your purchase, and you have had minimal access to the content.
  • Payment Processing Failure: If funds were deducted but the course access was not granted due to a technical issue on our platform.

3. Course Quality and Accuracy

Disclaimer: While MentorStreak takes measures to ensure course quality and prevent misleading course descriptions, we are not responsible for:

  • The accuracy, completeness, or usefulness of course content.
  • Whether a course meets your specific learning objectives or expectations.
  • The teaching style, pace, or quality of instruction provided by the creator.
  • Any educational outcomes or results promised by the creator.
  • Technical issues with course content that cannot be resolved within a reasonable timeframe.

Students are responsible for reviewing course previews, descriptions, reviews, and creator credentials before purchasing. If you have concerns about course content or quality, please contact our support team, and we will investigate and work with the creator to address legitimate issues.

4. Refund Request Process

If you believe you qualify for a refund under the limited exceptions listed above, please follow these steps:

  • Contact MentorStreak support within 14 days of your purchase with detailed information about why you believe you qualify for a refund.
  • Provide evidence of the issue (e.g., screenshots of duplicate charges, proof of unauthorized access, technical error documentation).
  • MentorStreak will review your request and respond within 10 business days.
  • If your refund is approved, funds will be returned to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

5. Refund Denials

Refund requests will be denied if:

  • You have accessed a substantial portion of the course content (more than 25%).
  • Your request is submitted more than 14 days after purchase.
  • You are requesting a refund due to personal circumstances, change of mind, or dissatisfaction.
  • You are disputing the course quality or educational value.
  • The course was purchased with fraudulent intent.

6. Subscription Plans and Memberships

If MentorStreak offers subscription or membership plans with access to multiple courses:

  • Subscription fees are non-refundable.
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings.
  • Upon cancellation, your premium access will continue until the end of your current billing cycle.
  • You will not be charged for the next billing cycle after cancellation.
  • No pro-rated refunds will be issued for the remaining unused portion of your subscription term.

7. Free Trials

If you signed up for a free trial of a course or subscription:

  • You must cancel before the trial period ends to avoid being charged for a paid plan.
  • Once the trial ends and payment is processed, the standard No Refund Policy applies.
  • Charges for trial to paid conversion cannot be refunded.

8. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you dispute a course purchase through your payment provider (credit card chargeback, PayPal dispute, etc.):

  • MentorStreak will investigate the chargeback and defend against it if the purchase was legitimate and authorized by you.
  • If your account is used for fraudulent chargebacks, your account will be suspended and you will be banned from the platform.
  • You are liable for chargeback fees associated with fraudulent disputes.
  • Filing a chargeback does not override this refund policy.

9. Creator Refunds Policy

For course creators on the platform:

  • If a refund is issued to a student due to an exception listed in this policy, the commission paid to the creator will be reversed or deducted from future payouts.
  • Creators are not entitled to additional compensation if refunds are issued due to their course content or quality issues.
  • MentorStreak reserves the right to remove courses or suspend creators if refund rates become excessive.

10. Modifications to This Policy

MentorStreak reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Your continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. If you do not agree with the policy changes, you may stop using the platform.
